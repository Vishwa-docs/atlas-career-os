The insight

The missing layer is longitudinal.

  • One portable Career Graph — skills, achievements, trajectory — owned by the person and carried across every employer and decade.
  • It compounds for 40 years: phases, plateaus, pivots — not a pile of disconnected applications.
  • Legible to all three sides at once — candidate, employer, university — over the same graph.

A person at 22 and at 52 is the same evolving node — Atlas keeps that thread unbroken, and turns daily work into verifiable, navigable proof.

AI craft

Azure GPT-4o, behind a swappable contract.

One LLMClient interface

Chat · streaming · structured outputs · embeddings. Azure in prod, a deterministic mock in tests & offline — provider-swappable, never coupled.

Structured & grounded

Pydantic-validated JSON via constrained decoding. RAG over the candidate's graph + jobs + Malaysian wage data — answers cite their sources.

Composite client

Real GPT-4o for generative calls; deterministic embeddings so vector search stays consistent with the corpus. Best of both, zero drift.

Guardrails

Untrusted content fenced against prompt-injection · PII redaction · per-org token & cost metering · retries with backoff.

Matching, done honestly

Where you're heading — weighted in.

  • Hybrid retrieval: BM25 keyword + pgvector semantic, fused with Reciprocal Rank Fusion.
  • Trajectory-aware: blends semantic fit, skill overlap, transition-graph feasibility, and salary fit.
  • Two-sided & explainable: a slightly-under-qualified candidate on a strong arc can rank above an exact-keyword match — and the Glass Box says why.
